# Dashboard Features & Insights
**1. Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment Year**

- Visualizes sales growth over time.
- Peak sales observed around 2018 ($0.20M), followed by stabilization.
- Helps understand outlet maturity vs revenue contribution.

**2. Sales by Item Type**

- Top-performing categories:
  - Fruits & Vegetables
  - Snack Foods
  - Household Items
- Lower contribution categories highlight potential optimization opportunities.

**3. Fat Content Analysis**

- Comparison between Low Fat and Regular items.
- Regular fat items contribute a larger share of total sales.
- Useful for product mix and health-focused strategy decisions.

**4. Sales by Outlet Size**

- Medium-sized outlets contribute the highest sales (~42%).
- Small and High outlets show comparatively lower contribution.

**5. Sales by Outlet Location Type**

- Tier 3 cities generate the highest revenue.
- Tier 1 cities show lower contribution, indicating untapped growth potential.

# Business Insights

- Medium-sized outlets and Tier 3 locations are key revenue drivers
- Snack foods and daily essentials dominate sales
- Outlet expansion strategy should prioritize emerging cities
- Stable ratings across outlets indicate consistent customer experience
